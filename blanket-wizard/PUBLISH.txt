STEP 1: Open Replit Publishing Tool
   • Look at the LEFT SIDEBAR in Replit
   • Click "All tools" (at the bottom)
   • Click "Publishing"

STEP 2: Choose Static Deployment
   • Click the "Static" option
   • Click "Set up your published app"

STEP 3: Configure the Deployment
   • Public Directory: blanket-wizard/dist
   • Build Command: (leave empty - already built!)
   • Click "Publish"

STEP 4: Wait & Done!
   • Replit will deploy your wizard
   • You'll get a live URL like: your-app.replit.app
   • Share it with anyone!
